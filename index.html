Core Innovations

7 technologies no competitor has.

Each product solves a specific bottleneck in the current industry. Together they form an unbreakable competitive moat.

🤖
MesmamBot Autonomous Field Robot

A low-cost, UV-LED equipped ground robot that roams desert terrain at night. Scorpions fluoresce bright blue-green under UV light — our computer vision system identifies them from 5+ meters. The robot captures using a safe pneumatic mechanism, containers the scorpion, and logs GPS coordinates, species prediction, and capture time. Workers stay home. Robots work all night.
