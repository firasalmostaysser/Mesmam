# Mesmam مسمام

**The Sting That Saves Lives · لسعة تنقذ الأرواح**

Mesmam is a Tunisian deep-tech startup building the world's first full-stack infrastructure for the global scorpion venom industry — from AI-powered autonomous collection robots to a certified B2B global marketplace.

## Files

- `index.html` — Landing page (open in any browser)
- `BUSINESS_ANALYSIS.md` — Full strategic analysis, market research, competitor landscape, legal roadmap, and 30-day action plan

## The Opportunity

Scorpion venom is priced at **$39 million per gallon** — the most expensive liquid on Earth. Yet the entire industry runs on manual labor, informal collection, and zero technology. Tunisia is home to *Leiurus quinquestriatus* (Deathstalker) — the world's most medically valuable scorpion species.

Mesmam owns the full vertical stack:
1. **Scout & Collect** — MesmamBot (autonomous UV-guided night robot) + community collector app
2. **Extract & Process** — AutoMilk Station (automated, AI species-aware extraction)
3. **Certify & Authenticate** — VerifyVenom™ (portable spectrometer + ML Certificate of Analysis)
4. **Sell Globally** — Mesmam Marketplace (B2B SaaS platform)

## The Vision

Scorpion venom is the most expensive liquid on Earth, yet the industry operates with dangerous manual methods, zero technology, and devastating human cost. Mesmam brings deep tech to every step:

- **Autonomous UV Hunting Robots** — AI-guided nocturnal robots using UV-blue light to detect fluorescent scorpions safely
- **Automated Venom Extraction** — Robotic milking systems processing 150+ scorpions/day
- **AI Species Classification** — Computer vision for instant species ID, population counting, and yield estimation
- **Global B2B Marketplace** — Digital platform connecting verified suppliers to pharma labs worldwide
- **Portable Venom Analyzer** — Field-grade spectrometry for quality testing
- **Rural Empowerment** — Fair-trade model creating dignified tech jobs for underserved communities
